DIGITAL MARKETING

DESCRIPTION

Digital marketing encompasses all marketing efforts that use an electronic device or the internet. Businesses leverage digital channels such as search engines, social media, email, online advertisements, and other websites to target a larger segment of prospective customers who are most likely to purchase or employ their product/service.
